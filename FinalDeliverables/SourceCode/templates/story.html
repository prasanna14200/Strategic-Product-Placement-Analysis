Findings

Key Story Findings

Front of Store Dominates

Products positioned at the front of the store consistently achieve higher average sales volumes compared to aisle and end-cap placements.

Promotions Drive Growth

Promoted products show a significant sales lift, with promotional campaigns increasing average sales volume across all categories.

Demographics Matter

Consumer demographics play a critical role in sales patterns, with certain segments showing stronger purchasing behavior.

Foot Traffic Correlates

Higher foot traffic areas correlate with increased sales, suggesting strategic placement in high-traffic zones is crucial.
